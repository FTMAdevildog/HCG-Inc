Commitment

The Hernandez Consulting Group (HCG) is a collaborative consulting group consisting of an experienced team that is committed to supporting all safety net organizations. HCG’s mission is to strengthen its clients’ organizational capacities to respond to a continually transforming health care and human services environment, while upholding a genuine commitment to the communities it serves.

Leadership

HCG supports organizational leadership through critical stages of development with an understanding that health care and wellness services are transforming amid a sleuth of regulatory standards, value-based payment reform, and competitive service and funding priorities. HCG is committed to propelling its clients into the next stage of readiness while remaining focused on quality and positive health outcomes. HCG promotes an organization’s critical thinking, clarifies complex issues, and leverages its assets to create and implement sustainable and flexible solutions.

Development

HCG is prepared to provide assessment on various stages of an organization’s development, and engages in competitive strategic planning, development of continuous performance improvement, program design, and key technical assistance.
